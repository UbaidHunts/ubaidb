CV and Research Overview

ubaidb@umd.edu
Office: IRB 3256

I am an Electrical and Computer Engineering Ph.D. candidate at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am a part of Computer Architecture and Systems Lab (CASL) and is working under the supervision of Dr. Bahar Asgari. My research focuses on optimizing resource utilization in sparse applications to accelerate the throughput and latency in various computing architectures, including CPUs, GPUs, and FPGAs. My detailed research overview can be found here. I secured my bechelors in Electrical Engineering from Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) in May, 2022.
